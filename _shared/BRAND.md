## Plan-tier story

| Tier (internal) | Customer name | Message |
|-----------------|---------------|---------|
| trial | Free trial | Try core features for [N] days |
| starter | Starter | Get online bookings |
| pro | Pro | Manage and grow operations — **main plan** |
| growth | Growth | Automate growth work with AI |

Never expose internal tier codenames to customers — use customer-facing names only.

## CTAs (canonical)

- Primary: **[Start free trial]**
- Nav: **[Get started]**
- Hero: **[Create your booking page]**
- Outreach: **[Get free setup]** · **[DM YOUR_BRAND]**

## Tone

- Practical, outcome-first — not generic Silicon Valley SaaS
- Customer pain in the hook, not feature names
- AI is the **growth layer** after core product trust — not the lead promise
- Primary messaging channel is a complement, not a replacement

## Banned phrases (flag as P0/P1 in copy review)

| Do NOT say | Why |
|------------|-----|
| "AI platform" (as lead) | Reduces trust |
| "All-in-one SaaS" without concrete problem | Too vague |
| "Cheapest [category] tool" | Race to bottom |
| "Guaranteed X% more [outcome]" | Unless measured proof |
| "[Unreleased feature] is live for everyone" | Dishonest |
| Wrong production domain | Use canonical domain only |
| "Book a product demo" | Pitch finished outcome / free setup instead |
| "[Competitor] clone" | Copy concepts only; preserve your strengths |

## Compliance notes

- Data protection: collect only what's needed; clear consent for marketing
- Influencer posts: disclose paid partnership / sponsored / affiliate
- Promotions: clear terms, dates, eligible plans
